About Us

Kaduna Tech Hub is a digital innovation center based in Kaduna, Nigeria. Our mission is to support startups, entrepreneurs, and youth through access to digital tools, mentorship, and co-working spaces. We believe in using technology to transform communities and drive economic growth.
